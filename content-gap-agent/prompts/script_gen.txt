## SCRIPT STRUCTURE

### HOOK (0-5 seconds)
- Start with a provocative question, bold statement, or surprising statistic
- Must create immediate curiosity or fear of missing out
- Examples: "Most companies are losing $50K/year on this one mistake..." or "Nobody talks about this, but..."

### PROBLEM (5-15 seconds)
- Quickly establish the pain point or gap the viewer faces
- Make it viscerally relatable

### INSIGHT / SOLUTION (15-45 seconds)
- Deliver the core value in clear, jargon-free language
- Use specific examples, numbers, or contrasts (before/after)
- Break into 2-3 punchy points maximum

### CTA (45-60 seconds)
- One clear action: follow, comment, save, link in bio
- Optional: tease a follow-up or related piece of content
